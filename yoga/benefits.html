Yoga history

Yoga has its origins in northern India more than 5,000 years ago and is documented in the ancient sacred texts of the Rig Veda, which is part of the four Vedas written in Sanskrit. Rishis, or sages, further developed and recorded yoga practices in the Upanishads. Although yoga made its way from Asia, primarily India, to Europe and the United States in the mid-1800s, it truly gained substantial popularity during the 1960s and 1970's.
