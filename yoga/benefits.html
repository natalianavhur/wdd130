Yoga history

Yoga, rooted in northern India, dates back over 5,000 years. It migrated from Asia to Europe and the US in the mid-1800s, carried by scholars exploring Eastern philosophies. However, its notable popularity blossomed during the 1960s and 1970s in the West, driven by its perceived holistic benefits. This surge in interest propelled yoga into the mainstream, integrating it into modern lifestyles globally. With its rich history and benefits, yoga has transformed into a revered global practice for physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.
